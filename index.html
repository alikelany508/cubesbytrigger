Frequently Asked Questions

Is the system approved by the Zakat and Income Authority?

Cube Accounting Systems are approved by the Authority.

Are the systems related to the Transport Authority regarding the car rental system?

The systems are related to the Transport Authority.

Does the system work in two languages?

Yes, the system operates in both Arabic and English and can be extended to other languages.

Is it possible to customize the format of reports, invoices, and printouts in the system?

Yes, you can customize the format of system output to suit your company's needs.

Is there an option to export reports to MICROSOFT OFFICE?

Yes, all reports can be exported to Microsoft Office.

Is it possible to manage multiple facilities in the system?

Yes, you can manage multiple facilities within the accounting system, each with its own outputs.

Is it possible to print reports spanning previous years to the current year without leaving the current fiscal year?

Yes, it is possible to print reports spanning multiple fiscal years.

Are there alerts in the system?

Yes, there are alerts in the accounting system, such as alerts for accounts showing unusual activity or negative balances. There are also alerts in the human resources system to notify of document expiration within a specified period set by the user, as well as alerts related to inventory, expired items, suspended customers, and exceeding credit limits.
